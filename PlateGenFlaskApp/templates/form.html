Plate Generator

(A simple cpu-only front-end)

Generates 128x32 greyscale images of Indian Car Number Plates using a pre-trained GAN.

Input number (required) and state (optional). Leaving it blank will pick state codes at random.

Example: '6' ; 'OD' creates a .zip of 6 images like this one.

Developed as a project-based-learning exercise during my internship at CDAC, Pune to fulfill the requirements of Practice School I.
